# Caption contract

- Keep timestamps monotonic and non-negative.
- Prefer one complete phrase per cue; split long speech at natural boundaries.
- Preserve meaningful pauses, interruptions, music, and sound cues when required.
- Mark unintelligible speech rather than inventing words.
- Add speaker labels only when identity is evidenced.
- Keep a correction ledger for names, acronyms, and domain terms.
- Treat forced alignment as a separate optional stage; the researched MMS aligner is CC-BY-NC-4.0 and is not part of the approved production route.
