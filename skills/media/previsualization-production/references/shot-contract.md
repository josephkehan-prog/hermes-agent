# Shot ledger contract

Record per shot:

- stable `shot_id` and parent scene;
- narrative purpose and emotional beat;
- subject/action, framing, angle, lens feel, camera movement, and blocking;
- screen direction, eyeline, geography, props, wardrobe, and lighting continuity;
- planned duration, transition, dialogue/audio cue, and caption dependency;
- board path, motion-proof path, model, prompt, seed, dimensions, and frame count;
- accept/reject decision and production risk.

Use the smallest proof that resolves the uncertainty. A beautiful unrelated
clip is a failed previs result.
